Hello!

My name is Alana Cronin and I am a Sound Designer based in Inner West Melbourne. I am passionate about creating sound effects and ambient soundscapes for digital media. I am proficient with many sound design programs including Reaper, Logic and Audition and can produce high quality work that is both immersive and engaging.

I have a deep appreciation for the intricacies of sound, whether it's the subtle sound of raindrops or the grandeur of a cinematic explosion, I love the details. I graduated in 2024 with a Bachelor of Design (Digital Media) from RMIT, which has taught me many different digital media disciplines (including web design to make this portfolio) and helped me learn how to effectively create sound design for many types of digital media. I design well in teams and individually, and use an empathetic approach to create specifically tailored sound design for each client.

If you would like to collaborate or have any questions at all, please feel free to get in touch.
